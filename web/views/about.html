What do we do?

HSCS.io empowers passionate computer science high school students to enrich computer science learning in their communities. We will do this by aiding student run events, educating students in CS, and enabling students to run their own events.

What is a CTF?

CTF stands for "Capture the Flag". CTFs are a type of computer science competition. Certain pieces of information, called "flags", are placed on servers, encrypted, hidden, or otherwise stored somewhere difficult to access. During the competition, different challenges are released which allow the participants to reverse engineer, break, hack, decrypt, and do whatever it takes to capture that flag for points.
